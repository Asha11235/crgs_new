    The Department of Social Services (DSS) is one of the leading Government Departments of People's Republic of Bangladesh under the Ministry of Social Welfare.The Department of Social Services was estab lished in 1961.

    The mandate of the Department of Social Services is to provide services to the vulnerable groups of society, the poorest of the poor, the marginalized and the disadvantaged groups. Our service goes further than just bringing services to these groups.

    About NCTF

    NCTF are the central actor of the initiative who are supported by the Village Education Resource Center (VERC), Center for Services in Disability (CSID), Mass-line Media Center (MMC), mPower Social En terprise and with overall coordination from Save the Children. Save the Children is supporting this initiative under ICT & Innovative Partnership project: responding to well-known challenges forchildren in new ways.
